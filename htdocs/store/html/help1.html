    Why Register at Store


     By choosing to register at the Store you can:

  • Receive a better price for some of our products.
  • Check status and content of multiple orders, both current and past with full security
  • Add items to your shopping cart to purchase them altogether and often save on shipping charges. You can also leave them in your shopping cart to return later with all information retained.
  • Update your account information including shipping and billing addresses, forms and types of payment tools including credit card numbers.
  • Add items to your wish list for purchase at a later time or to email the list to your partner.

          Please note that you do not need to register to use our site. However, you do have to register to place an order. By choosing to do so, you will also be able to take advantage of the additional benefits we will be adding as we continue to develop our site.
